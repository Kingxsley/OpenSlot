Cookie Policy

Last updated: [date]

This Cookie Policy explains how Enjeeoh uses cookies and similar technologies. We keep this deliberately minimal: we use only strictly necessary cookies that are required to sign you in and protect your session. We do not use advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking cookies.

What cookies we set

CookiePurposeTypeExpiry
os_tokenKeeps a member signed in to their dashboard.Essential, HttpOnly~7 days
os_consoleKeeps a platform/super admin signed in to the console.Essential, HttpOnly~7 days
csrfAnti-forgery token checked on requests that change data, to prevent cross-site request forgery.Essential~7 days

Why we don’t show a cookie banner

Under UK/EU rules (and similar laws elsewhere), strictly necessary cookies — those required to deliver a service the user has requested, such as authentication and security — do not require consent. As we use only such cookies, no consent banner is needed. If we ever introduce non-essential cookies, we will update this Policy and ask for consent first.

Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings. Because our cookies are essential, blocking them will prevent you from signing in and using the dashboard or console.

Other storage

Enjeeoh does not store your session token in browser local storage; the session lives only in the HttpOnly cookie above, which page scripts cannot read.
