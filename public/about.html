About Enjeeoh

Scheduling should not be a cost non-profits carry.

Enjeeoh is booking software made for NGOs and non-profits. It is free for verified organisations and kept running through donations, so the organisations doing the hardest work never have to pay to be reached.

Why we built it

Enjeeoh started with a simple frustration. A friend running a small community charity was spending a meaningful slice of a tiny budget on tools that larger companies barely notice, scheduling among them. Every dollar going to software was a dollar not going to the people the charity existed to help.

So the idea was straightforward. Take the kind of booking tool that usually sits behind a per-seat subscription, build it properly, and make it free for the organisations that need it most. Open the source so anyone can read it, host it, or improve it. Keep it alive through donations rather than fees.

What we stand for

Free for NGOs, always

Every feature is included for verified non-profits. There is no paid tier and no upsell.

Built for non-profits

Made specifically for the organisations that need it, not adapted from a corporate tool.

Your data is yours

You host it, you own it. No selling of data, no tracking of the people who book with you.

Funded by goodwill

Donations cover hosting and development, so the service can stay free for everyone.
